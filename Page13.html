Prometheus Flight Controller

The flight computer is a custom STM32F446-based avionics system developed for an L2 certification and thrust-vector-controlled rocket, integrating an IMU, altimeter, high-g accelerometer, GPS, and LoRa telemetry. It supports onboard flash and microSD data logging, multiple pyro channels for deployment events, and PWM outputs for thrust vector control and roll stabilization, with robust power management provided through both battery and USB-C input. The overall design prioritizes reliability, modularity, and expandability, making it suitable for both certification flights and active guidance and control testing.
