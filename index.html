About Me

Hello, my name is Dorsheed Abdalla and I am an Electrical Engineering student at Ontario Tech University. I am a hardworking and adaptable learner who thrives on new challenges and continuous skill development. My technical strengths include PCB design, embedded systems, control systems, and RF communication, with hands-on experience designing custom STM32-based hardware, developing firmware, and building PID control systems in MATLAB/Simulink. I am proficient with tools such as Altium Designer, Bluebeam Revu, and SolidWorks, and programming in C++, Java, and Arduino/ESP32 environments. My interests lie in embedded hardware, power electronics, and signal processing, and I have practical experience through internships, rocketry, and racing projects. Alongside my technical expertise, I hold certifications in safety and high-power rocketry, and I value teamwork, communication, and problem-solving in all projects I take on.
