Kwyeter IoT Project

In the process of designing a compact, rechargeable, low-cost noise meter measuring dBA/dBC sound levels. It implements wireless transmission of processed sound data to a an app (no raw audio) utilizing the ESP32 Wi-Fi MCU with sound sensors or I2S microphones. The live metrics on an app allows those with hearing disabilities know the sound-levels within a given public space before arriving for safety. The design emphasizes low power consumption, portability, and ease of installation. Firmware leverages ESP32 hardware, IoT/cloud platforms, and ESP32 development resources.
