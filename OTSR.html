Nov 2024 - Present

Ontario Tech Space & Rocketry - Avionics Member

As a member of the Ontario Tech Rocketry Team, I served on the avionics subteam where I designed a custom STM32-based telemetry PCB for the SRAD avionics stack, integrating the RFD900 RF module, dual CAN bus transceivers, and onboard SD and flash memory for long-range telemetry and data logging. I implemented a 4-layer PCB with a signal–ground–power–signal stackup, applying impedance control, ground stitching, and robust power regulation to support RF transmission and multiple peripheral interfaces, while also contributing to embedded firmware development using STM32CubeIDE. In addition to hardware design, I supported board assembly, rework, and validation using THT/SMD tools and electrical probing, and assisted with launch-day avionics checks and troubleshooting for the team’s two-stage rocket at the Launch Canada competition in Timmins; I now continue to support the team in a mentorship role, guiding newer members on avionics design, testing practices, and system integration.
