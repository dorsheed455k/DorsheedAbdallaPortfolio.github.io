June 2024 - July 2025

Thrust Vector Control Rocket System

As a member of the Ontario Tech Racing hardware and electronics team, I designed and assembled a combined Tractive System Status Indicator (TSSI) and Ready to Move Light (RML) PCB used for critical vehicle safety indication. Using Altium Designer, I integrated high- and low-voltage isolation, optocouplers, comparators, and 555-timer–based logic to meet FSAE safety requirements. The design incorporated onboard power regulation, fault detection, and light control circuitry, along with dedicated test points and a dimmer section to support debugging and iterative testing. I supported prototype validation by integrating the PCB with the vehicle’s lighting system and verifying performance through breadboard trials, oscilloscope measurements, and thermal checks under full operation. In addition, I assisted with vehicle wiring and electronic integration within the EV top hat and supported pre-competition rehearsals and on-site activities during the FSAE competition in Michigan.
